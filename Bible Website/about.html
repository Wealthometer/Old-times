Our Story

Our founding team at the 2020 launch event

Divine Word was founded with a simple yet profound vision: to create a digital platform that brings the timeless wisdom of the Bible to people around the world in an accessible, engaging, and meaningful way.

Our journey began in 2020 when a group of developers, theologians, and educators came together with a shared passion for scripture and technology. We recognized that while the Bible has been translated into countless languages and distributed globally, many people still find it challenging to engage with its content in our fast-paced digital world.

We set out to build a platform that would not only provide the text of the Bible but also offer tools for deeper study, understanding, and application. Our goal was to create an experience that would be valuable for seasoned scholars, new believers, and curious seekers alike.

Bible Translations

Divine Word offers multiple Bible translations to help you engage with scripture in the way that speaks to you best.

KJV

King James Version

First published in 1611, the KJV is one of the most influential and widely read Bible translations in English, known for its majestic style and historical significance.

ESV

English Standard Version

Published in 2001, the ESV is an "essentially literal" translation that emphasizes word-for-word accuracy while maintaining readability in contemporary English.

NIV

New International Version

First published in 1978 and updated in 2011, the NIV balances accuracy with clarity and is one of the most popular modern English translations.

NLT

New Living Translation

The NLT uses dynamic equivalence to create a translation that is easy to read and understand while maintaining biblical accuracy.

NASB

New American Standard Bible

Known for its commitment to literal translation, the NASB is valued by those seeking a close rendering of the original biblical texts.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Divine Word free to use?

Yes, Divine Word is completely free to use. Our mission is to make the Bible accessible to everyone, so we don't charge for access to the scriptures or basic study tools.

How do you ensure the accuracy of the Bible text?

We source our Bible texts from reputable publishers and organizations that maintain the integrity of each translation. Our team of biblical scholars also reviews the content to ensure accuracy.

Can I use Divine Word offline?

Currently, Divine Word requires an internet connection to access all features. However, we're developing an offline mode that will allow you to download and read the Bible without internet access. Stay tuned for updates!

How can I contribute to Divine Word?

There are several ways to contribute! You can provide feedback on the platform, help with translations, or support us financially. Visit our "Get Involved" page to learn more about how you can be part of our mission.

Is my data secure when I use Divine Word?

Yes, we take data security seriously. Your notes, highlights, and personal information are encrypted and stored securely. We never share your data with third parties without your explicit consent. You can read more in our Privacy Policy.
