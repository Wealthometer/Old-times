Sermon on the Mount

Matthew 5:1-12 May 15, 2023

The Beatitudes describe the character of those who belong to God's kingdom. Jesus begins with "Blessed are the poor in spirit" - those who recognize their spiritual poverty and need for God.

Fruit of the Spirit

Galatians 5:22-23 April 28, 2023

Paul lists nine attributes of the fruit of the Spirit: love, joy, peace, patience, kindness, goodness, faithfulness, gentleness, and self-control. These are not separate fruits but different aspects of the same fruit.
